CHARK by protzz

A chess roguelike about risk, synergies, and precise calculation.

Game Overview

CHARK is a unique blend of chess and roguelike mechanics. Build combos from piece-cards, take two moves per round, and survive the enemy’s response. Your mission is to hit the score target to advance. Every decision matters: tempo, position, and resource management all shape your survival.

How to Play

  • Each round presents three cards — play only two.
  • Click a card to reveal all legal moves on the 5×5 board.
  • Shift, set up an exchange, or force a tactic — then enemies advance toward you.
  • End your turn in danger and you lose a life.

Progression & Upgrades

After each level, earn money and bonuses for unused hands. Visit the Shop to buy Artifacts — powerful upgrades that bend the rules in your favor. Discover synergies, unlock new strategies, and keep advancing as the challenge escalates.
